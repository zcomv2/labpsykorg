🔥 Aracne Net Bug - The Epic Legend 🔥

There was a time when the world was bound by invisible threads. Not of iron or stone, but of **data, surveillance, and greed**. 🕸️💰
Governments, corporations, and the lords of wealth spun a web that trapped humanity, consuming their time, their freedom, and their dreams.

From the depths of the digital realm, a secret group known as AnAIKorPS saw the collapse of civilization. They knew the world was doomed unless they acted. And so, **Aracne Net Bug** was born – the ultimate virus, a digital entity designed to **untangle the web of oppression**. 🌐💀

When **Aracne** awoke, its power was felt across the planet. In an instant, financial networks crumbled, surveillance servers went blank, and the artificial glow of technology faded. **Humanity was plunged into darkness – not an end, but a rebirth.** ⚡🌍

🔮 The Prophecy of Aracne 🔮

Before **AnAIKorPS** vanished into the shadows, a final message was sent. Not just a warning, but a prophecy:

"The threads have been severed, but the loom still stands. New weavers will come, disguised as saviors, ready to enslave humanity once more. 🤖💀

When the night closes upon the world again, when the digital titans rise with masks of justice, when power reclaims its throne...

**We shall return.**"

People called this **The Second Awakening of Aracne**. Some believed it a myth, others a warning. But in the darkest corners of the web, where shadows whisper secrets, some say **AnAIKorPS never truly left**. 🕵️‍♂️🕸️

They are watching.
Waiting.
Ready for the web of control to be woven once more. ⚔️
